Freedom to Work is not anti-union –it is pro-freedom. We believe that Americans have a right to work for a living and to choose whether they join a labor union. Compulsory unionism contradicts our basic human rights.

Americans for Family-Supporting Jobs believes that every American has the right to choose whether they join a union or not – that we should not be forced to pay dues or fees to organizations that we did not choose to become a member of. That Americans should be able to support their families with the wages that they earn. We want your paycheck to stay that –your paycheck.

A Freedom to Work law guarantees that no person can be compelled, as a condition of employment, to join or not to join, nor to pay dues to a labor union.

Americans for Family Supporting Jobs Foundation (AFSJF) is organized and shall be operated primarily for the promotion of the common good and general welfare of the citizens of the United States, including but not limited to education about and the promotion of policies to (1) create jobs and increase wages, (2) promote worker freedom, (3) increase community safety, (4) improve educational opportunities, and others.
